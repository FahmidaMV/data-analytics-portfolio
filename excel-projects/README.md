# **Shopping Trends & Customer Behaviour Dashboard (Excel)**


## *Overview*
This project analyzes customer shopping trends and behaviour using Microsoft Excel.


## *Objective*
To understand customer purchasing patterns and identify key insights for business decision-making.


## *Tools Used*
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Data Cleaning
- Charts & Dashboard Design


## *Dataset*
Customer shopping dataset containing:
- Product categories
- Sales data
- Customer demographics
- Payment methods


## *Dashboard Features*
- Total Sales, Orders, Customers & Ratings
- Sales by Product Category
- Revenue by Region
- Sales Trend Over Time
- Customer Segmentation (Age, Gender)
- Payment Mode Analysis


## *Key Insights*
- Fashion and Electronics generate high revenue
- Most customers fall in 26–35 age group
- Online purchases dominate over in-store
- Sales fluctuate monthly with peak periods
