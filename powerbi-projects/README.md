# **Marketing & Sales Impact Analysis-Supplement Industry (Power BI)**


## *Overview*
This Power BI dashboard analyzes marketing spend and its impact on sales performance in the supplement industry.


## *Objective*
To evaluate ROI, customer growth, and marketing effectiveness.


## *Tools Used*
- Power BI
- Data Modeling
- DAX
- Data Visualization


## *Dataset*
Includes:
- Marketing spend data
- Sales revenue
- Customer data
- Product categories


## *Dashboard Features*
- Total Revenue, Marketing Spend, ROI
- Sales by Region
- Revenue vs Marketing Trend
- ROI by Product Category
- Customer Segmentation
- Conversion Analysis


## *Key Insights*
- High ROI from specific product categories
- Strong correlation between marketing spend and revenue
- Certain regions outperform others
- Customer retention trends visible
